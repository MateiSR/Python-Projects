reminders made right

Elephant is a reminder app designed for students who have a hard time dealing with their school schedule.
Our idea was to make an app which allows students all over the world to keep track of their classes.

Get started

App Interface

The interface is intuitive and easy to use.

First off, when you start the app, it will check for reminders that have went off while you were offline. You will be noticed about them, and they will be deleted afterwards.

This is the main app. Here you can easily add reminders by providing a title, the date & time, and optionally a description.

The news & stats window provides a small list of the trending news regarding the COVID-19 pandemic, as well as current available statistics provided by the World Health Organization.

The use of the View reminders is straight-forward. This provides a list of the currently active reminders, and a listbox from which you can delete any reminder you wish to.
